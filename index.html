Description

Ph-client is a chromium based client with built-in TOR proxy for anonymous surfing on such sites, like [ROSCOMNADZOR]. As this client uses TOR, your ISP will not be able to block, intercept or read any web traffic between your favorite site and your computer. Moreover, client have cookies enabled, means that it can store your login data on your machine if you need to. This project does not collect any data at all (it couldn't be anonymous otherwise) - without telemetry!

Anonymously

Your internet service provider can not know what you're watching, because ph-client use TOR and TOR use encrypted channels

Faster

At present, TOR is the fastest anonymous network, they speed more 30 mbit/seconds - it's good for HD

Open Source

You can watch for development, change the code, add suggestions and make your own build on github.
